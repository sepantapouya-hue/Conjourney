On the roadmap

Three lifecycle emails exist as templates but raise NotImplementedError

  • Pre-flight Report — "Pre-flight: {X} / {Y} checks passed"
  • Weekly Insight — "Insight: {headline}"
  • Milestone — "🎉 {N} questions answered — that's about {H} hours saved"
What the shopper never sees
  • No GDPR / cookie consent UI in the widget
  • No business-hours awareness — widget always says it's ready
  • No proactive email capture beyond the optional pre-chat survey
